ABOUT US

Zekmos Hatchery and Farms A non-Profit volunteer group of farmers in poultary breeding located in Ghana, precisely Tamale. We are specialize in breeding and hatching high-quality, healthy chicks and poultry from a variety of breeds for backyard backyard farmers. We use state-of-the-art incubators and brooders to ensure optimal conditions for hatching and early chick development. Our eggs are carefully selected and stored under optimal conditions prior to incubation, and we monitor temperature, humidity, and other environmental factors closely throughout the incubation process. Once hatched, chicks are kept in a temperature-controlled brooder until they are ready to be sold. SERVICES

Our Service

Zekmos Hatchery and Farms locally incubate and hatch fertilized eggs od clients in a controlled environment to ensure optimal temperature, humidity, and ventilation to maximize hatching rates. We have a hatching rate of over 90% Once eggs are hatched, Zekmos Hatchery and Farms care for and nurture the chicks until they are strong enough to be sold or start to lay their own eggs. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms provide newly hatched chicks to customers, typically farmers or hobbyists looking to raise their own poultry. We also offer consulting services to help customers choose the right breeds of chickens, provide guidance on proper care and feeding, and answer any questions related to raising chickens. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms also sell equipment related to egg hatching and chick rearing, such as incubators, brooders, feeders, and waterers. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms may offer transport services to deliver the chicks to their destination. We also offer comprehensive set of services to help customers start their own chicken flocks, whether for commercial or private/ personal purposes.

Why do business with us?

High hatch rates: Zekmos Hatchery and Farms uses advanced incubation techniques and carefully monitors environmental conditions to ensure a high hatch rate, which means customers are more likely to successfully hatch healthy chicks. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms carefully selects quality breeding stock to ensure highest-quality eggs, which can lead to healthier and more productive chicks. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms offers a wide variety of Guinea fowls and chicken breeds, allowing customers to choose the type of chicken that best suits their needs and preferences. We offer competitive prices for high-quality eggs, making it an affordable option for people who want to hatch their own chicks. Hatching eggs can be a fun and educational activity for children and adults alike, providing a hands-on opportunity to learn about the life cycle of chickens and the importance of sustainable agriculture. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms provides personalized services to each customer, taking the time to understand their needs and preferences and providing customized recommendations.
