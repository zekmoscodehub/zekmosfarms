Welcome to Zekmos Hatchery and Farms

Your one-stop destination for poultry products, hatching services, and farm supplies in Ghana.


Our main area of business is poultry hatching, breeding, and the sale of poultry birds, with a special focus on guinea fowls. The company also deals in the supply of agricultural inputs, including day-old chicks, turkeys, poultry drugs, feeders, drinkers, and other related poultry and crop products. Additionally, Zekmos Hatchery and Farms offers commercial hatchery services to both large-scale and backyard farmers in the poultry industry.We also sell sophysiticated computer controlled incubators of all sizes and capacities.
