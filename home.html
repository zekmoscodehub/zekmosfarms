ABOUT US

Zekmos Hatchery and Farms A non-Profit volunteer group of farmers in poultary breeding located in Ghana, precisely Tamale. We are specialize in breeding and hatching high-quality, healthy chicks and poultry from a variety of breeds for backyard backyard farmers. We use state-of-the-art incubators and brooders to ensure optimal conditions for hatching and early chick development. Our eggs are carefully selected and stored under optimal conditions prior to incubation, and we monitor temperature, humidity, and other environmental factors closely throughout the incubation process. Once hatched, chicks are kept in a temperature-controlled brooder until they are ready to be sold. Breeds We offer a variety of chicken and turkey breeds, including: Broilers Layers Heritage breeds Dual-purpose breeds Turkeys

SERVICES

Our Service

Zekmos Hatchery and Farms locally incubate and hatch fertilized eggs od clients in a controlled environment to ensure optimal temperature, humidity, and ventilation to maximize hatching rates. We have a hatching rate of over 90% Once eggs are hatched, Zekmos Hatchery and Farms care for and nurture the chicks until they are strong enough to be sold or start to lay their own eggs. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms provide newly hatched chicks to customers, typically farmers or hobbyists looking to raise their own poultry. We also offer consulting services to help customers choose the right breeds of chickens, provide guidance on proper care and feeding, and answer any questions related to raising chickens. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms also sell equipment related to egg hatching and chick rearing, such as incubators, brooders, feeders, and waterers. Zekmos Hatchery and Farms may offer transport services to deliver the chicks to their destination. We also offer comprehensive set of services to help customers start their own chicken flocks, whether for commercial or private/ personal purposes.

How to Start Your Own Hatching Business

Are you planning on starting your own poultry egg hatching business? If yes, then Zekmos Hatchery is the answer. We manufacture small and medium-large scale commercial egg incubators ideal for entrepreneurs who want to start their own egg hatcheries.

Why start a hatchery?

It is a highly profitable business and your return on investment is very quick. The poultry industry is one of the fastest-growing sectors in the world, and Ghana is no exception. Chicken meat demand is growing so fast that farmers are unable to satisfy the market demand. Therefore, poultry production and business are the way to go.

What facilities do I need?

Our incubators and hatchers need to be stored indoors with good airflow and a temperature between 15 and 25 degrees Celsius. This ensures optimal functioning.

How much space do I need?

This depends on the size of your hatchery. We can help you determine the required space. Many customers use a spare room, garage, or shed for their incubators.

Who do I sell my newly hatched chicks to?

You will sell your day-old chicks to "growers" who rear the chicks to fully grown birds. You can find growers by searching the internet for people who sell chickens.

How long does it take to start making money?

You can start making money in as little as 3-4 weeks. Your first batch of chicks will hatch after 21 days, and you can sell them for profit immediately.

How much money can I make in a year?

This depends on the size of your hatchery. Generally, you can make back your investment within 12 months with an 80% hatch rate.

What if I need help after I start?

We pride ourselves on excellent after-sales service. Contact us anytime for advice before or after your purchase. We are here to help you succeed.
