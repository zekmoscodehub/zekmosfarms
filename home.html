First fact

Guinea keets are hardy and can adapt to various climates, making them an ideal choice for backyard farmers.

Second fact

They are excellent foragers, feeding on insects and grasses, which makes them easy to feed and maintain.

Third fact

Guinea fowl are known for their alarm calls, making them excellent watchdogs, and can also help control pests and insects in the garden.
