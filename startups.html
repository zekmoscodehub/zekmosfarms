What equipment do I need?

What facilities do I need?

Our incubators and hatchers need to be stored indoors with good airflow and a temperature between 15 and 25 degrees Celsius. This ensures optimal functioning.

How much space do I need?

This depends on the size of your hatchery. We can help you determine the required space. Many customers use a spare room, garage, or shed for their incubators.

Who do I sell my newly hatched chicks to?

You will sell your day-old chicks to "growers" who rear the chicks to fully grown birds. You can find growers by searching the internet for people who sell chickens.

How long does it take to start making money?

You can start making money in as little as 3-4 weeks. Your first batch of chicks will hatch after 21 days, and you can sell them for profit immediately.

How much money can I make in a year?

This depends on the size of your hatchery. Generally, you can make back your investment within 12 months with an 80% hatch rate.
